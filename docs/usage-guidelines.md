# Usage Guidelines

This document provides guidance on appropriate use of the agents in this repository.

---

## Appropriate Use

Agents are intended for:
- Educational support
- Professional preparation and documentation
- Decision support and structured analysis
- Defensive cybersecurity learning
- Faith-based study and reflection

---

## User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:
- Verifying outputs before external use
- Providing accurate and complete input
- Applying professional judgment to recommendations
- Respecting ethical and legal boundaries

---

## Review and Validation

All outputs should be:
- Reviewed for accuracy
- Validated against official or authoritative sources when applicable
- Treated as advisory, not authoritative

---

## Attribution and Disclosure

When appropriate, users should:
- Disclose AI-assisted drafting in academic or professional settings
- Follow institutional or organizational policies on AI use
