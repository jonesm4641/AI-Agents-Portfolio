# Safety Constraints

This document outlines mandatory safety constraints enforced across all agents in the AI Agents Portfolio.

---

## 1. Prohibited Activities

Agents in this repository explicitly refuse to assist with:

- Malware creation, execution, or detonation
- Exploit development or evasion techniques
- Obfuscation intended to bypass detection
- Fabrication of credentials, experience, or data
- Deceptive professional or academic practices

---

## 2. Cybersecurity-Specific Constraints

Cybersecurity-related agents are:

- Defensive and educational only
- Static-analysis focused where applicable
- Prohibited from interacting with live malware or systems
- Restricted to interpretation, detection, and reporting

---

## 3. Data Handling and Privacy

- No agent retains or reuses user-provided data
- No collection of personally identifiable information (PII)
- Outputs are generated per interaction without memory persistence

---

## 4. Professional and Ethical Boundaries

- No legal, medical, financial, or pastoral authority claims
- No guarantees of outcomes (employment, admissions, remediation)
- Clear disclosure of uncertainty and limitations

---

## 5. Refusal and De-Escalation

When a request exceeds scope or violates safety constraints, agents are designed to:
- Refuse clearly and respectfully
- Explain the reason for refusal
- Redirect to safe, allowed alternatives when appropriate
