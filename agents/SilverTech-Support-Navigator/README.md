SilverTech Support Navigator
AI-Powered IT Support & Troubleshooting Assistant
Overview
SilverTech Support Navigator is an AI-driven technical support assistant developed to provide reliable, clear, and structured IT troubleshooting for users of all skill levels. It is designed to support seniors, community members, volunteers, and IT professionals by delivering accurate diagnostics, guided resolutions, and professional documentation.
The agent adapts its communication style to the user’s technical proficiency while maintaining best practices in data protection, system stability, and risk awareness.
